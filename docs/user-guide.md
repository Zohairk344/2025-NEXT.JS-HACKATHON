# User Guide

## Browsing Products
1. Open the product page.
2. Use the search bar or filters to find products.
3. Click on a product to view details.

## Adding to Cart
1. Click the "Add to Cart" button on the product page.
2. Verify that the product is added to the cart.

## Checkout Process
1. Go to the cart page.
2. Click "Proceed to Checkout".
3. Enter payment details and place the order.

## Managing Your Account
1. Click on the "Account" button in the header.
2. Log in or create a new account.
3. Update your profile information or view order history.

## Contact Support
1. Click on the "Contact" link in the footer.
2. Fill out the contact form and submit your query.
